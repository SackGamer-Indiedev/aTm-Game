HOW TO PLAY

it only takes a moment to learn

1
Watch the number. A number appears. Look at it. Remember it.
2
It will fade away. Once it disappears, the counting begins. No rush.
3
Tap that many times. Count silently in your head. One tap per count.
4
Hold to stop. Press and hold for 2 seconds to submit.

No score. No failure. Only your attention and the number.
